Given a text file file.txt, print just the 10th line of the file.

Assume that file.txt has the following content:

Line 1
Line 2
Line 3
Line 4
Line 5
Line 6
Line 7
Line 8
Line 9
Line 10

Your script should output the tenth line, which is:

Line 10

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Ans

#Solution One:
#head -n 10 file.txt | tail -n +10

#Solution Two:
#awk 'NR==10' file.txt

#Solution Three:
sed -n 10p file.txt
